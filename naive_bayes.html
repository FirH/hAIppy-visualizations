Naive Bayes based Spam Detector

How does it work?
The AI has seen many emails before. It learned that certain words (like "FREE" or "winner") appear much more often in spam, while others (like "meeting" or "report") are normal, and saves it in form of probability distributions. When new email is being added, it looks at each word and asks: "Does this word make it more or less suspicious?". It then combines all probabilities of spam and normal words to make a final decision.
