💡 What's happening here?
Logistic Regression is a model that answers yes/no questions using probabilities rather than absoulte answer. If you give it examples, such as "failed after spending 2 hours of study" or "passed after spending 6 hours of study", it finds a S-shaped curve that best fits your data. It tells the probability of passing given the amount of study hours, not just a "yes" or "no"
